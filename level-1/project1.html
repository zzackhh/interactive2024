Welcome.

I love technology. I was born in 2002, before the iPhone and the iPad.

But trust me, I grew up on the internet.

I grew up in an interesting time, where tech was rapidly changing; throughout my childhood, consoles came and went, and devices like my DS with a Game Boy slot or websites like Tumblr fueled my creativity. This collection is a digital archive of those relics of history that we might forget –– but are certainly integral to many of our lives.

The iPod Nano (2005–2017)

Apple today introduced the iPod® nano, a revolutionary full-featured iPod that holds 1,000 songs yet is thinner than a pencil and smaller than competing players.

The iPod nano features an ultra-portable design, a gorgeous color screen, and Apple's patent pending Click Wheel. It syncs with the iTunes® Music Store, the world’s leading digital music service, and comes in white or black models.

According to Steve Jobs, "iPod nano is the biggest revolution since the original iPod," as it transformed the portable music market with its innovative design and ease of use.

With its optional accessories like lanyard headphones and colorful protective cases, the iPod nano became both a fashion and tech icon.

By Apple Press Release, 2005.
